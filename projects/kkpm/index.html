Maestro of the Year

KKPM choir Musical Director, Maestro Aris Macandog celebrated his 40th birthday last March 17, 2017 at the American Polish Community Center, in Troy MI. Aris’ parents, sister and brother-in-law, relatives, friends and guests of no less than our own Msgr. John Vargas as well as visiting priest from Malaybalay, Bukidnon, Fr. Elrich Jorquia, and the KKPM family came to this surprise birthday celebration. Attendees braved the rain and snow that night, who all came early before Aris was ushered into the hall to surprise him. Thumbs up to the organizers who successfully kept the preparations top secret to Aris until the actual celebration.

Flores de Mayo

May 6, 2017

Flores de Mayo is a festival held in the Philippines in the month of May. It is one of the May devotions to the Blessed Virgin Mary and lasts for the entire month. Start your celebration of Flores de Mayo with the KKPM at St. Rene Goupil at 7:00 pm!


Annual Fun Day

May 20, 2017

Each year we visit our Dominican nuns at their monastery with mass in the morning followed by a potluck lunch. After that, we give our youth their Annual Fun Day event to show how much we appreciate all their hard work and passion they have given to KKPM and all its events.
